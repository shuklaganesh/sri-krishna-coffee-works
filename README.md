# Sri Krishna Coffee Works - Digital Transformation Roadmap

**Since 1953. Brewed with legacy. Carried forward with love.**

## Project Overview

This is a comprehensive digital transformation project plan for Sri Krishna Coffee Works, a legacy filter coffee brand based in Hyderabad, India. The project aims to modernize the brand's digital presence while honoring its 70+ year heritage.

### Key Metrics
- **Timeline:** 6-8 weeks
- **Budget:** ₹1.03L - ₹1.38L
- **Phases:** 7 major phases
- **Target Launch:** May 2026

## Project Scope

1. **Brand Foundation** - Logo, brand identity, and style guide
2. **Packaging Design** - 250g coffee pouch design with FSSAI compliance
3. **E-Commerce Store** - Fully functional Shopify store
4. **Google My Business** - Local SEO and GMB optimization
5. **Content & SEO** - Website content and search optimization
6. **Workflow Automation** - Email marketing and customer automation
7. **Launch & Marketing** - Official launch and initial campaigns

## About Sri Krishna Coffee Works

Established in 1953 on R.P. Road, Secunderabad, Sri Krishna Coffee Works has been serving authentic South Indian filter coffee for over seven decades. Known for live grinding in front of customers, the brand represents tradition, quality, and the timeless craft of filter coffee.

**Brand Vision:** To keep the timeless craft and tradition of authentic South Indian filter coffee alive, creating cups that generations will continue to cherish.

**Brand Mission:** To serve thoughtfully crafted coffee that brings comfort, nostalgia, and tradition into every cup, offering people a small pause from life and leaving a warm memory of our brand.
